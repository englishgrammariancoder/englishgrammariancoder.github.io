Introduction

The aim of ENGLISH GRAMMARIAN CODER is to prepare boys & girls students well equipped with scientific knowledge, who will face the challenges of the modern world in a befitting manner. It lays special emphasis on character building education convent style and infuses in its student all those qualities which lead them to become useful citizen of this Islamic republic.

ENGLISH GRAMMARIAN CODER is a private institution and runs. On non-commercial and non-political basis. It has well equipped science labs and above all has The services of highly qualified and devoted lectures. It is housed in a spacious building situated in the heart of KARACHI
