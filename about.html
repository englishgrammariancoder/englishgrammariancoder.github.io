HISTORY

Iqra Intermediate College for boys & girls was founded on 10, June 1990. By Mr. Saleem Ahmed Khan and under the supervision Iqra Educational Society. The College was setup to promote and accelerate the higher education of the students of Karachi. It was started as the Intermediate College, the founder of the college was appointed its first principal Ms. Naheed
Ms. Naheed seeking to provide better facilities for the students she arrange the all science labs, common rooms, and library. She also keeping with the tradition of provide us a holistic education stress is laid on ensuring a value added academics curriculum is taught and students are provided to participate in a variety of co-curricular activities that serve their development of ethical person, Independent thinkers and responsible and active citizens. Throughout its history the students of Iqra Intermediate have demonstrated outstanding performance obtaining position in the Intermediate Board of Karachi in all the programs of study offered by the college. Their outstanding performance has also been reflected in various curricular and co-curried activities i.e. Arts, Painting On the sports field the college has been a regular recipient of the general championship of inter collegiate tournaments winning the cricket, football through ball and athletic competition.

PRINCIPAL

To the students and faculty :

There is no greater calling than to teach young people.

This college will no longer honor race or ethnicity. The only identity this college will recognize is your individual identity your character, your scholarship, your humanity. And the only national identity this college will recognize is Pakistani and Iqra Intermediate College is created to make better Pakistani. I am not interested in what your native language is? No obscene language will be tolerated anywhere on this college's property.

I am orienting the college toward academics and away from politics and propaganda. No more time will be devoted to tobacco, caffeine, or global warming. No more classes will be devoted to health issue. And there will be no more attempts to convince you that you are a victim because you are not white or male or have rich background. We will have failed if any one of you passes from this college and does not consider him or herself inordinately lucky to be alive and to be a Pakistani.

Now, please stand and join me in the Pledge of Allegiance to the flag of our country. And promise yourself that you will have to be a responsible citizen wherever you will be, as you are representing Pakistan.
